# Comprehensive Shopping Centre Research Prompt

Use this prompt with an AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) to conduct deep research on a specific retail location. Replace `[LOCATION_NAME]` and `[CITY]` with the actual location details.

---

## Research Prompt

```
I need you to conduct comprehensive research on the following UK retail location:

**Location:** [LOCATION_NAME]
**City:** [CITY]
**County:** [COUNTY] (if known)
**Postcode:** [POSTCODE] (if known)

Please research this location systematically and provide all available information in the following structured format. For each section, indicate if information is unavailable or uncertain.

---

## 1. BASIC INFORMATION

**Location Name:** [Exact official name]
**Type:** [SHOPPING_CENTRE / RETAIL_PARK / OUTLET_CENTRE / HIGH_STREET]
**Full Address:** [Complete street address]
**City:** [City name]
**County:** [County/region]
**Postcode:** [UK postcode]
**Coordinates:** [Latitude, Longitude - if you can find them]

---

## 2. CONTACT INFORMATION

**Phone Number:** [Main contact number]
**Official Website:** [Full URL]

---

## 3. OPERATIONAL DETAILS

**Opening Hours:** [Day-by-day schedule, or general hours]
**Year Opened:** [Year the centre first opened]
**Number of Stores/Units:** [Total retail units]
**Number of Floors:** [How many levels]
**Total Floor Area:** [Square feet or square meters]
**Retail Space:** [Square feet or square meters - retail-specific area]
**Parking Spaces:** [Total car parking capacity]
**Parking Price (0-2 hours):** [£X.XX - short-stay parking cost]
**EV Charging Available:** [Yes/No]
**EV Charging Spaces:** [Number of EV charging points]
**Public Transit Access:** [Nearest stations, bus routes, etc.]
**Anchor Tenants Count:** [Number of major anchor stores]

---

## 4. OWNERSHIP & MANAGEMENT

**Owner:** [Property owner/landlord company]
**Management Company:** [Centre management/operating company]

---

## 5. FOOTFALL & PERFORMANCE

**Annual Footfall:** [Number of visitors per year, if available]
**Number of Retailers:** [Current retailer count]

---

## 6. TENANT DIRECTORY

Please provide a complete list of all current tenants/stores. For each tenant, include:
- Store/Brand Name
- Category (e.g., Fashion, Food & Beverage, Electronics, Health & Beauty, etc.)
- Subcategory (if applicable)
- Unit Number (if known)
- Floor/Level (if known)
- Anchor Tenant Status (Yes/No - major brands like Primark, M&S, Debenhams, etc.)

Format as a numbered list or table.

---

## 7. SOCIAL MEDIA PRESENCE

**Instagram:** [Full Instagram URL]
**Facebook:** [Full Facebook page URL]
**YouTube:** [Full YouTube channel URL]
**TikTok:** [Full TikTok profile URL]
**Twitter/X:** [Full Twitter/X profile URL]

---

## 8. ONLINE REVIEWS & RATINGS

**Google Rating:** [X.X out of 5.0]
**Google Review Count:** [Number of reviews]
**Google Total Votes:** [Total votes/ratings]
**Facebook Rating:** [X.X out of 5.0]
**Facebook Review Count:** [Number of reviews]
**Facebook Total Votes:** [Total votes/ratings]

---

## 9. COMMERCIAL METRICS (if available)

These may be harder to find, but include if you discover them:

**Health Index:** [Score if available]
**Largest Category:** [Dominant retail category]
**Vacancy Rate:** [Percentage]
**Vacant Units:** [Number]
**Average Tenancy Length:** [Years]
**Percentage Multiple Retailers:** [%]
**Percentage Independent Retailers:** [%]

---

## 10. DEMOGRAPHIC DATA (Catchment Area)

Research the surrounding area demographics:

**Population:** [Catchment area population]
**Median Age:** [Years]
**Families Percentage:** [%]
**Seniors Percentage (65+):** [%]
**Average Household Income:** [£X,XXX]
**Income vs National Average:** [% above/below]
**Homeownership Rate:** [%]
**Homeownership vs National:** [% above/below]
**Car Ownership Rate:** [%]
**Car Ownership vs National:** [% above/below]

---

## 11. ADDITIONAL RESEARCH NOTES

Include any other relevant information discovered:
- Recent news or developments
- Upcoming openings/closures
- Special features or amenities
- Historical context
- Notable facts or statistics
- Sources used for verification

---

## RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

Please use multiple sources:
1. Official website (if available)
2. Google Maps / Google Business Profile
3. Wikipedia
4. News articles
5. Property/retail industry databases
6. Social media profiles
7. Review sites (Google Reviews, Facebook)
8. Local council/planning documents (if accessible)
9. Retail directories and industry publications

For each piece of information, note your confidence level:
- ✅ High confidence (verified from official source)
- ⚠️ Medium confidence (from reliable secondary source)
- ❓ Low confidence (estimated or unverified)

---

## OUTPUT FORMAT

Please provide your research in a clear, structured format matching the sections above. Use markdown formatting for readability. If information is unavailable, clearly state "Not found" or "Unavailable" rather than leaving blank.
```

---

## Usage Instructions

1. **Copy the prompt above**
2. **Replace placeholders:**
   - `[LOCATION_NAME]` - e.g., "Lakeside Shopping Centre"
   - `[CITY]` - e.g., "Grays"
   - `[COUNTY]` - e.g., "Essex" (optional)
   - `[POSTCODE]` - e.g., "RM20 2ZP" (optional)

3. **Paste into your AI assistant** (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, etc.)

4. **Review and verify** the results, especially:
   - Cross-check critical numbers (parking spaces, store counts)
   - Verify website URLs are correct
   - Confirm coordinates if provided
   - Validate tenant lists against official directories

5. **Update your database** with the verified information

---

## Tips for Best Results

- **Be specific:** Include as much initial information as you have (postcode helps a lot)
- **Ask follow-ups:** If something is unclear, ask the AI to dig deeper
- **Verify sources:** Check the AI's sources, especially for financial/commercial data
- **Use multiple queries:** Break complex research into focused follow-up questions if needed
- **Request citations:** Ask the AI to cite sources for verification
