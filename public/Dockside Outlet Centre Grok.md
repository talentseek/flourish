\- Recent developments: McDonald’s opened 2024/2025; Burger King closed early 2025; Peacocks closed 2024\.    
\- Ongoing asset management focuses on “outlet \+ community” mix (retail \+ services \+ leisure).    
\- Adjacent amenities: Odeon Cinema, restaurants, marina, Historic Dockyard Chatham.    
\- Parking became paid in 2023 (previously free); charges apply after initial free period (exact short-stay rates via operator site).    
\- Sources used: Official website docksideshopping.com (primary), Kent Online news archives, TripAdvisor, Zapmap, WD Ltd statements, Google Maps/Business Profile, retail industry reports.
