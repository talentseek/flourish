## 11. ADDITIONAL RESEARCH NOTES

- Dockside is a converted Grade II listed former boiler house.  
- Opened 2003, refurbishment cost approx. £67m.  
- WD Ltd purchased the site in 2015 for £9.1m.  
- Footfall exceeded pre‑COVID benchmarks by 8.5% (per CACI).  
- Westbrooke Alternative Asset Management supplied £10m senior debt facility in 2024.  
- Mix increasingly includes community services (nursery, job centre, dentist).  
- Soft play (556 sq m) approved 2020 but unclear if implemented.  
- Drive-in cinema proposal rejected in 2021.
