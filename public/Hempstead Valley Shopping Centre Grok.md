\#\# 11\. ADDITIONAL RESEARCH NOTES  
\- Opened 17 October 1978 as Kent's first out-of-town shopping centre; modelled on North American malls with UK's second food court (originally "Picnic Parlour").    
\- Major extensions: 1990s (M\&S addition), 2015 ("The Venue" dining), ongoing multimillion-pound refurbishment (2020s) including new signage and Home Bargains opening November 2025 in former BHS unit.    
\- Special features: Free parking (6 hours), wheelchair/scooter hire, baby changing/feeding rooms, CCTV-monitored car parks with Park Mark award, events space.    
\- Recent news: Home Bargains 20,000 sq ft store opening 2025; emphasis on family/leisure destination.    
\- Historical context: Started as 250,000 sq ft district centre; now key Medway retail hub competing with Bluewater etc.
